Legazpi, Philippines- In preparation for the Hackathon this June, the BicolIT.org brings you TechTalk 5 on May 10,2014 1-5 pm at Parole and Probation Office, DOJ , Penaranda Park, LegazpiCity.Techtalk is an event conducted by the BicolIT.org which aims to provide students or even professionals and teachers knowledge about the new trends in technology. It is often given for free.

The event will cater talks about Twitter Bootstrap, Full-stack Web Development Technologies, Ruby on Rails and Why Should I Build My Own Startup? And other Basics I need to know? Those will be given by some of the expert members of the organization and other technologically inclined speakers. Techtalk will also be joined by some of the known computer expert members of the BicolIT.org.

A day full of fun and learnings awaits those who are going. BicolIT.org also gives freebies to the attendees. Come and join us as we unfold the new trends of Technology.


Here are the awesome speakers for our Tech Talk 05:

John Kevin Marbella Basco Web Developer & Designer, Mayon Volcano Software

John is also the founder of Laravel Snippets, a repository of code snippets for the Laravel PHP Framework, he will discuss Twitter Bootstrap.

Magno Edilberto R. Conag III CEO, NUECA

Magno is a homegrown Technology Entrepreneur based in Naga City, having founded Nueca in 2011, he will talk about Why Should I Build My Own Startup? And Other Basics I Need to Know.

Ryan Yonzon Web Development Engineer & Open Source Advocate

Ryan will discuss Full-stack Web Development Technologies.

Richard Gonzales Ruby on Rails Developer

Richard will discuss Ruby on Rails

About Us

Bicol Information Technology Organization is a community of IT professionals who are from and are in the Bicol Region, Philippines. Our primary objective is to share our expertise, conduct regular meetups in Legazpi City, Metro Manila and hopefully soon in Naga City and the rest of the region.

We conduct regular technology talks for free for students and professionals to augment the training needs and expose others to the latest trends in information technology.
